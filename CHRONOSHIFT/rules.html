Rules


Every single expedition offered by Chronoshift Expeditions follows this set of rules, with some additions our staff will inform you of on certain expeditions.


•Stay on the path
•Do not startle the wildlife.
•Do not collect anything whatsoever.
•Do not touch anything that is not permitted.
•Stay with your guide at all times unless told to do otherwise.
•Going against these rules may result in death or severe punishment.
